Resort Accommodations

The resort has 3 levels of accommodations

  1. Peasant: $999.99
  2. King: $1999.98
  3. God Emporer: $999.99 per hour

Peasant Accommodations:

  • 1 twin sized bed
  • 1 window conviently located in the bathroom facing the jungle, not the beach.
  • free continental breakfast located at the nearest continent.
  • MUST BE AVALIABLE IF GOD EMPORER NEEDS ANYTHING.
  • Free wifi.
  • No toilet paper provided.*

King Accommodations:

  • 1 king sized bed
  • 2 windows, both conviently located in the bathroom facing the jungle, not the beach.
  • free continental breakfast and lunch located at the nearest continent.
  • MUST BE AVALIABLE IF GOD EMPORER NEEDS ANYTHING.
  • Free wifi.
  • Toilet paper included

God Emporer Accommodations

  • 14 king sized beds pushed together to make 1 giant bed.
  • No windows because the entire building is a giant window.
  • Free breakfast, lunch, and dinner delivered via helicopter.
  • Everyone else on the island must do anything you say.**
  • must pay for wifi.
  • Toilet paper not included.
*Any guest below GOD EMPORER level cannot import or purchase toilet paper.
**cannot make king level guests share toilet paper.
