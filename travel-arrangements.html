Travel Arrangements

How to get to the island

Peasant:

Swimming from the nearest continent. (Roughly 14 shark infested miles)

King:

Moterboats will leave from the second nearest continent at 12am sharp. (Roughly 397 miles)

GOD EMPORER:

Helicopter will pick you up from your house only stopping on nearest continent for shark bait and drinks, so you can enjoy the view of the peasants suffering as they swim towards the island.
